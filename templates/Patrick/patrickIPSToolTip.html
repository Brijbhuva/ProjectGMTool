Pat's Information Processing Style

Pat leans towards a comprehensive information processing style when he needs to gather information to problem-solve. That is, before following some option that seems promising, he first gathers information comprehensively to try to form a complete understanding of the problem before trying to solve it. Thus, his style is "burst-y"; first he reads a lot, then he acts on it in a batch of activity.
