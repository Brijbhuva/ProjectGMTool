Pat's Attitudes About Risk
Pat's risk-aversion leads her to mainly use software features that she is familiar with and to avoid features that she hasn't used before. Pat is risk averse when she uses computers to perform tasks. When confronted with new software features, Pat worries that she will spend time on them and not get any benefits from doing so. She prefers to perform tasks "the safe" (ie, familiar) way if possible, even if less familiar features might promise a more direct solution.
