Welcome to the GenderMag tool, a tool for using the GenderMag Method. The GenderMag Method (short for “Gender Inclusiveness Magnifier”) is a method for finding gender-inclusiveness issues in software features. It is an analytical process and materials to support that process. A team of software developers and/or user experience (UX) professionals can use it to evaluate their software products from a gender-inclusiveness perspective. To find out more about where GenderMag came from, or how to do it, to get the kit.
